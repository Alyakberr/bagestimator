1. Information We Collect

1.1 Log Data

When you visit BagEstimator.com, our servers and hosting infrastructure automatically record certain information that your browser sends, commonly referred to as "log data." This may include your Internet Protocol (IP) address, browser type and version, the pages of our Service that you visit, the time and date of your visit, the time spent on those pages, referring or exit URLs, and other diagnostic data.

1.2 Device and Viewport Specifications

Because the Service is engineered as a mobile-first, responsive calculator intended for use on job sites, we and our service providers may collect device-related information, including device type, screen resolution, viewport dimensions, operating system, mobile network carrier information, and input method (touch versus pointer). This data is used strictly to optimize layout rendering, ensure numeric keypad triggers function correctly on mobile devices, and to maintain Core Web Vitals performance standards. We do not use this data to construct a persistent profile identifying you personally outside of the advertising functions described in Section 4.

1.3 Cookies and Local Storage Data

We and our third-party partners use cookies, pixel tags, local storage, and similar tracking technologies to collect and store information about your visit, as detailed further in Section 3.

1.4 Information You Provide Voluntarily

The calculators on BagEstimator.com operate entirely client-side. Project dimensions, material selections, and other figures you enter into the calculators are processed locally in your browser and are not transmitted to, stored on, or logged by our servers unless you separately contact us, for example via email, in which case we retain whatever information you choose to share in that communication.

2. How We Use Information

We use the information described above for the following purposes:

  • To operate, maintain, and improve the performance, speed, and reliability of the Service;
  • To diagnose technical problems and monitor for malicious or abusive traffic patterns;
  • To understand aggregate usage trends through analytics tools;
  • To allow third-party advertising networks to serve and measure advertisements, including personalized advertisements, as described in Section 4;
  • To comply with applicable legal obligations, enforce our Terms of Service, and protect the rights, property, and safety of BagEstimator, our users, and the public.

3. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

A cookie is a small data file placed on your device that allows a website or a third party to recognize your browser. BagEstimator.com uses the following categories of cookies:

  • Strictly Necessary Cookies: Required for the basic functioning of the Service, such as remembering your unit preference (Imperial versus Metric) during a session.
  • Performance and Analytics Cookies: Used to understand how visitors interact with the Service so that we can improve usability and page speed.
  • Advertising Cookies: Placed by third-party advertising networks, including Google AdSense, to deliver relevant advertising and measure campaign effectiveness, as described in Section 4.

You can instruct your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent. However, if you do not accept cookies, some portions of the Service, particularly interactive calculator features that rely on session storage, may not function properly.

BagEstimator.com is a free-to-use Service supported in part by advertising revenue. We display advertisements served by Google AdSense and may, at our discretion, display advertisements from alternative or supplemental advertising networks.

4.1 Use of the DART Cookie and Behavioral Advertising

Google, as a third-party vendor, uses cookies, including what is historically referred to as the "DART cookie," to serve advertisements to visitors of the Service based on their prior visits to BagEstimator.com and other websites across the Internet. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve personalized, interest-based ads to you based on your visit to this and/or other sites you have visited, including behavioral profiling derived from your general web browsing history. Third-party vendors, including Google, may also use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our Service or other websites.

4.2 Personalized Versus Non-Personalized Advertising

Depending on your location and applicable consent requirements, advertisements served on the Service may be personalized (based on your interests and browsing behavior) or non-personalized (based only on contextual factors such as the content of the current page, general geographic location, and device type). Where required by law, including under the GDPR, we will obtain your consent before serving personalized advertising and will rely on a consent management platform to record and honor your choices.

4.3 Alternative Advertising Networks

In addition to Google AdSense, we reserve the right to work with alternative advertising networks and exchanges. Each such network may independently collect data through its own cookies and tracking pixels, subject to that network's own privacy policy. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any advertising network whose cookies appear on the Service.

5. Analytics

We use basic web analytics tracking scripts, including services such as Google Analytics, to collect aggregate, non-identifying statistical information about visitor behavior, including page views, session duration, bounce rate, traffic sources, and approximate geographic location derived from IP address. This data helps us understand which calculator tools are most useful to visitors and where the Service can be improved. Analytics providers may use cookies and similar technologies to collect this information, and their use of your data is governed by their own respective privacy policies.

6. Your Opt-Out Rights and Choices

You have several mechanisms available to limit or opt out of personalized advertising:

  • Google Ad Settings: You may opt out of personalized advertising served by Google by visiting Google Ad Settings and adjusting your preferences.
  • Network Advertising Initiative (NAI): You may opt out of personalized advertising from many participating ad networks simultaneously by visiting the NAI Consumer Opt-Out page.
  • Digital Advertising Alliance (DAA): United States visitors may also use the DAA WebChoices Tool to manage advertising preferences across participating companies.
  • Browser-Level Controls: Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies through their settings menus, and many offer "Do Not Track" signals, though such signals are not universally honored by all third parties.
Opting out of personalized advertising does not mean you will no longer see advertisements on BagEstimator.com. It means the advertisements you see will no longer be tailored to your interests and browsing history, and will instead be served based on contextual factors only.
